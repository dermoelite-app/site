Our Mission

Elevating Beauty,
Empowering People

DermoElite was born from a simple but powerful conviction: everyone deserves access to professional-grade beauty products and the knowledge to use them well. Too often, the world of premium skincare is locked behind salon doors or hidden in jargon-filled brochures.

We're changing that. Our platform brings together a curated marketplace of verified cosmetic brands, a comprehensive library of professional training courses, and intelligent tools that personalize every step of your skincare journey.

Based in Warsaw and built for Europe — and eventually the world — DermoElite Sp. z o.o. is registered under Polish law with a commitment to transparency, quality, and the highest standards of data protection.

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Controller Information

The data controller is DermoElite Spółka z ograniczoną odpowiedzialnością (DermoElite Sp. z o.o.), registered at ul. Grójecka 194/U12, 02-390 Warszawa, Poland. NIP: 7010995181, KRS: 0000856786. You may contact us regarding data protection matters at: legal@dermoelite-app.com

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity Data: name, username, date of birth
  • Contact Data: email address, postal address, phone number
  • Technical Data: IP address, device identifiers, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: pages visited, features used, course progress, purchase history
  • Profile Data: skin type preferences, product interests, survey responses
  • Biometric Indicators: skin analysis data submitted voluntarily via the app (anonymized and encrypted)

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal grounds under GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679):

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the services you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our platform, ensure security, and prevent fraud
  • Consent — for marketing communications and optional skin analysis features
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable Polish and EU law

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to: operate and improve the DermoElite platform; personalize product and course recommendations; process transactions and subscriptions; send service and marketing communications (with your consent); detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity; comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in platform operations (payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics) under strict data processing agreements. All third parties are required to respect the security of your data and to treat it in accordance with the law.

6. International Transfers

Where data is transferred outside the European Economic Area, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Account data is typically retained for the duration of your account plus 3 years for legal purposes.

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to: access your personal data; correct inaccurate data; request erasure ("right to be forgotten"); restrict or object to processing; data portability; withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at legal@dermoelite-app.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Polish supervisory authority (UODO) at uodo.gov.pl.

9. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies required for operation, and optional analytics cookies to understand usage patterns. You may control cookie preferences through your browser settings. A detailed cookie policy will be available at app launch.

10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include SSL/TLS encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Questions

About the App

When will the DermoElite app launch? +

We are targeting a public launch in 2026, following a closed beta phase in late 2025. Sign up to our newsletter to be notified the moment early access opens. We'll be launching simultaneously on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play).

Which countries will the app be available in? +

At launch, DermoElite will be available across the European Union, with a focus on Poland, Germany, France, and the Netherlands. Expansion to additional markets including the United Kingdom and North America is planned for subsequent phases.

Will the app be free to download? +

Yes, the DermoElite app will be free to download. The marketplace is open to all users. Premium training courses and advanced features such as AI skin analysis and expert consultations will be available under subscription or pay-per-use models. Pricing details will be announced before launch.

How does the skin analysis feature work? +

Our AI-powered skin analysis uses your device's camera and a series of guided prompts to evaluate your skin's characteristics — including hydration levels, tone uniformity, pore visibility, and sensitivity indicators. The results are processed on-device where possible, with your explicit consent required before any data is transmitted. Analysis data is never shared with third parties or used for advertising purposes.

Products & Brands

How are products selected for the marketplace? +

Every brand and product undergoes a thorough vetting process. Our team of dermatologists and cosmetic scientists review ingredient lists, safety certifications, and clinical evidence before any product is approved. We do not list products with prohibited substances under EU Cosmetics Regulation No 1223/2009.

Can I apply to list my brand on DermoElite? +

Yes. We welcome applications from professional cosmetic brands that meet our quality standards. Please contact us at partners@dermoelite-app.com with your brand information, product documentation, and any relevant certifications. Our partnerships team will respond within 5 business days.

Are products authentic and verified? +

Absolutely. We work exclusively with authorized distributors and brand-direct partnerships. Each product listing includes batch traceability, and our team conducts periodic spot checks. Counterfeit goods have no place on our platform — any suspected authenticity issue is investigated immediately.

Training & Education

Who creates the training content? +

All courses are created and reviewed by licensed professionals — including licensed estheticians, certified cosmetologists, and consultant dermatologists. We do not accept content from unverified influencers or anonymous sources. Every instructor profile is verified and publicly displayed on the platform.

Do I receive a certificate upon completing a course? +

Yes. Upon successful completion of accredited courses, you will receive a digital DermoElite Certificate of Completion. For certain professional programs, certificates will carry continuing education credits recognized within the EU beauty industry. Details will be available on each course listing at launch.

I'm a beauty professional — can I teach on DermoElite? +

We are building an instructor community and welcome applications from qualified professionals. Contact us at partners@dermoelite-app.com with your credentials, area of expertise, and a brief description of the course you'd like to offer. Our editorial team will review your application and guide you through the onboarding process.

Data & Privacy

How does DermoElite handle my personal data? +

We take data privacy very seriously. DermoElite is fully GDPR-compliant and operated under Polish law. Your personal data is never sold. We collect only what is necessary to provide our services, and you have full rights over your data at all times. Please see our Privacy Policy for complete details.

How can I delete my account and data? +

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. Once the app launches, this will be available directly within account settings. You may also submit a deletion request to legal@dermoelite-app.com. We will process your request within 30 days in accordance with GDPR Article 17.
